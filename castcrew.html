Cast & Crew


This performance of Romeo & Juliet consists of a small cast - 'Romeo' and 'Juliet' who are played by two professional dancers alongside a small crew who designed the shows set and interactive elements. The crew designed a series of dazzling light projections to act as the set background. The actors/dancers will interact with these projections.


"No more light will be shone"


Romeo - Robert
Juliet - Abby

'Romeo' is played by Robert. Robert is a professionally trained dancer based in Ireland. He has a particular interest in hip-hop and contemporary dance. Robert has been working within the industry for 4 years. This adaptation of 'Romeo & Juliet' combines Robert's dance disciplines harmoniously.
'Juliet' is played by Abby. Abby is also a professionally trained dancer; specialising in contemporary dance and ballet. Her debut performance was with The National Ballet of Ireland - Abby played the part of 'Odette', the Swan Princess in the performance 'Swan Lake' at the Bord Gais Energy Theatre.
