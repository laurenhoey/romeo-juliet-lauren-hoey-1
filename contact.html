1. Parking:
If you're travelling by car, parking is available on Dawson Street's Q-Park or on Grand Canal Square's Q-Park.

2. Public Transport:
If you are travelling by train, get off at Pearse Station. Merrion Square Park is less than a 5 minute walk from Pearse Station. If you are travelling by bus, 25, 66, 120 Dublin buses will leave just outside Merrion Square Park.


3. Directions:
Enter the park on the right hand side of Merrion Square. The entrance will be the second gate along the fence.
